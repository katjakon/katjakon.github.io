# Resources

Here are some resources you might find useful.

## Developing Skills for Alexa
If you are interested in programming skills for Alexa, you have to sign-up with an Amazon account and give additional information.

- [Amazon Developer](https://developer.amazon.com/)
- [Alexa Skills Kit Documentation](https://developer.amazon.com/en-US/docs/alexa/ask-overviews/what-is-the-alexa-skills-kit.html)

## Voice Assistants & Privacy Concerns
- The Guardian: [*'Alexa, are you invading my privacy?' – the dark side of our voice assistants*](https://www.theguardian.com/technology/2019/oct/09/alexa-are-you-invading-my-privacy-the-dark-side-of-our-voice-assistants)
- Mozilla Foundation: [*Privacy not included: Amazon Echo Dot*](https://foundation.mozilla.org/en/privacynotincluded/amazon-echo-dot/)

## Voice Assistants & LLMs

- The Decoder: [*Why Alexa still can't carry on fluent dialogues despite significant AI progress*](https://the-decoder.com/why-alexa-still-cant-carry-on-fluent-dialogues-despite-significant-progress-in-large-ai-language-models/)

## Designing Interactions with Voice Assistents
Some articles that talk about designing natural interactions with voice assistant.

- Google Design: [*Conversation Design: Speaking the Same Language*](https://design.google/library/conversation-design-speaking-same-language)
- Google Assistant: [*Learn about Conversation*](https://developers.google.com/assistant/conversation-design/learn-about-conversation)
- Amazon Developer: [*Design Your Skill*](https://developer.amazon.com/en-US/docs/alexa/design/design-your-skill.html)
- Mycroft AI: [*Voice User Interface Design Guidelines*](https://mycroft-ai.gitbook.io/docs/skill-development/voice-user-interface-design-guidelines)
